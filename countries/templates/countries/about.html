About the Game

On the homepage, you will see a randomly generated Country Name.


In the text bar underneath, enter your guess for the country's capital.


If you're correct, you will see a message with the country's correct capital name. If you are wrong, the page will refresh and you will have another chance to guess

THINGS TO NOTE:
  • Your guess must match the capital name exactly. This means you should write all guesses in title case (All word starting with Capital Letters)

  • The country generator may not send a new country each time. If this is the case, simply exit the url and return to it through localhost

  • You have unlimited tries on each country, but you cannot get to the next one without a correct answer

Enjoy the game, and good luck!
